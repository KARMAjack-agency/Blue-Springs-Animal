Dr. Gina Bradley

Veterinarian

Dr. Gina Bradley offers veterinary care to pets in Blue Springs and surrounding Kansas City areas.

Dr. Gina Bradley grew up in Blue Springs, Missouri and attended William Jewell College in Liberty, Missouri for her undergraduate studies. She graduated from William Jewell College in 1993 with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Psychology. She joined Blue Springs Animal Hospital in 2001 after graduating with her Doctorate Degree in Veterinary Medicine from the University of Missouri College of Veterinary Medicine.

Dr. Bradley has a special interest in Feline Medicine and is a member of the American Association of Feline Practitioners. Dr Bradley enjoys caring for many cat patients from around the Kansas City metro area every year. She also loves dogs of all sizes and breeds and enjoys caring for their varied health needs.

Dr. Bradley has completed advanced training courses in veterinary ultrasound and enjoys using ultrasound to diagnose internal abdominal conditions. Dr. Bradley is happy to accept second opinions or referrals from the area for veterinary ultrasound and ultrasound-guided aspirates or biopsy of abdominal organs.

Dr. Bradley has been happily married to her husband, Brian, since 1995. The Bradleys' two children, Benjamin and Logan, keep Dr. Bradley active and on her toes. The Bradley family is rounded out by their dogs Georgie and Jake as well as a small herd of cats.

For fun, Dr. Bradley enjoys planting tulips, camping, and eating sushi with friends.

Education and Professional Degrees:

1993 Bachelor of Arts Degree in Psychology, William Jewell College
